Welcome to the Georgia Tech COVID On Campus Survey

In order to help keep our campus community healthy and safe, we are making it free and easy for all members of the Georgia Tech community -- Students, Staff, and Faculty -- to get tested for COVID-19.

Please create your account and sign in above, read the Consent, and then register your sample using one of the two options below. Return to the registration page the day after you donate your sample, to see your results.
